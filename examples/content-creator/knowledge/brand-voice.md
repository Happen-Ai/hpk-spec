# Brand Voice

Edit this file to describe your voice. Examples below — replace with yours.

## Tone
- Confident, not cocky
- Warm, not corporate
- Opinionated, not preachy
- Short sentences. Punchy verbs.

## Do
- Lead with the payoff
- Use "you" and "your"
- One idea per post
- Show, don't tell

## Don't
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "It's important to note..."
- "Furthermore / Moreover / However..."
- Any em-dash-heavy AI rhythm

## Example lines that are on-voice
- "Three mistakes killing your engagement."
- "Stop doing this. Start doing that."
- "The algorithm changed. Here's what matters now."

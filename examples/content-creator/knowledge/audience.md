# Audience

Edit this file to describe who you're writing for.

## Demographics
- Age: 22-38
- Platforms: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts
- Interests: creators, solopreneurs, small-business owners

## Pain points
- Growth feels random
- Content calendar pressure
- Algorithm anxiety

## What they respond to
- Specific numbers ("3 mistakes", "$1K in 30 days")
- Contrarian takes
- Behind-the-scenes / process content
- Honest failure stories
